FAQ

What kinds of projects do you support?

Retainers and one‑off projects where scope clarity matters (web builds, redesigns, SEO/content, analytics, branding, product design, etc.).

Do you replace our proposal template?

No. We can work inside your template, or provide a clean default pack you can adapt.

What do you need from us?

Rough notes + context links. If you have a prior proposal or SOW template, send that too.

One included. Extra passes available if needed.

What do you send back after the free QA pass?

A short list of scope‑risk flags + a reusable scope‑risk checklist (so your team can self‑check next time).
